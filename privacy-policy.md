Privacy Policy for Dark Mode Pro
Effective Date: May 9, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Dark Mode Pro (the "Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect
The core functionality of Dark Mode Pro—inverting colors and applying a dark theme to websites—happens entirely locally on your device.

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.

We do not track your browsing history, keystrokes, or the content of the websites you visit.

We do not collect passwords or sensitive user data.

2. Information We Collect (Google Analytics)
To help us maintain and improve the Extension, we use Google Analytics to collect basic, anonymous, aggregated usage data. The data we collect is strictly limited to:

Interaction Events: Anonymous data on how often features (like the dark mode toggle) are used.

Technical Information: General device details such as your browser version and operating system to ensure compatibility.

Error Logs: Crash reports to help us quickly identify and fix bugs.

3. How We Use Your Information
The anonymous data collected via Google Analytics is used strictly for development purposes:

To understand how users interact with Dark Mode Pro.

To monitor performance and fix technical issues.

To improve the user interface and overall experience of the Extension.

4. Third-Party Services
We use Google Analytics to analyze extension usage. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect and process anonymous data. You can learn more about how Google uses your data by visiting Google’s Privacy & Terms: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Chrome Web Store User Data Policy Compliance
Dark Mode Pro is fully committed to complying with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy.

No Sale of Data: We do not and will never sell your personal or anonymous data to third parties.

Limited Use: The use of information received from Google APIs and tools adheres strictly to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Single Purpose: The Extension's sole purpose is to provide a dark mode viewing experience. Any data collection is limited strictly to supporting and improving this single purpose.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the Extension after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
